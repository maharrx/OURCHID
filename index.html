OURCHID

OU Research for Computer-Human Interaction Design

At OURCHID Lab, we are passionate about shaping the future of HCI through cutting-edge research and design. Our focus is on understanding the relationship between people and technology to design systems that solve problems in our society. As an interdisciplinary group, researchers in this lab engage in a variety of design research related to generative AI, wellbeing technology, conversational AI, and more.
